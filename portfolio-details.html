Augmented Reality


Augmented Reality (AR) is an interactive experience of combining a real-world environment with enhancements provided by computer-generated (CG) perceptual information. Unlike VR, AR is not a completely immersive, virtual experience. Virtual objects or displays lay over the real world with positional tracking, typically through a set of smart glasses, goggles, a monocle, or an HMD .This technology is especially useful for military and medical purposes as both require much focus and information in high-stress, timely scenarios. L&D understands the needs and challenges of working with AR. With tools like Unity, we will provide you with content that feels natural and realistic. Additionally, L&D can develop AR for all available headsets. Whether it is medical simulation, maintenance trainers, or enhancements, the limits of AR are boundless.
Our customized and technical expertise results in enhanced efficiency, profitability with a complete digital revolution. We deliver superior value for our customers in their digital journey with truly immersive experiences for a smart future. We special in defence training simulators - Unarmed high-risk simulation, virtual naval simulation, virtual flight simulations. From that day on we have consistently delivered several challenging projects for defence clients.
Lux & Decibels team is a multidisciplinary team of Engineers, Technologists, Artists, Senior Digital Experts and Consultants with many years of experience across various trades. We participate in listening to your briefs and challenges in order to evaluate and design solutions in a complete and sustainable package for a brightertomorrow. One of our first project with Virtual Reality Operator Training Simulator (VR-OTS) was PISTOL AND ASSAULT RIFLE FIRING RANGE for the armed forces.

Simulators


Virtual reality (VR) uses a simulator to immerse users in a real-time 3D virtual world, creating a truly unique experience. Potential customers have the opportunity to interact with your products as if they were actually present to showcase your innovations (whether industrial or medical), teach clients to handle, install or maintain your products, etc. A 3D simulator combines 3D images, Real Time 3D and a graphics interface with automated elements, taking account of ergonomic considerations and supervision requirements.It may take the form of glasses or of a full headset, with or without hand control, or even extend to a structure with actuators to simulate movement or vibrations.
Virtual Reality, Augmented Reality, VR Simulation, AR Simulation, Industrial Simulation, Mechanical Simulation, Precision 3D modeling is one of L&D’s key strengths. It could be 3D modeling of industrial machinery and components, products or anything else which has to be shown virtually with life-like reality. Our 3D designers are well versed with mechanical, architectural and other types of drawings. L&D uses high-end computers for high speed rendering with new generation modeling tools and applications.
With Virtual Reality Simulator Solutions, you can now test your team’s ability and limits in any terrain, surrounding or scenario without putting them in harm’s way. Bid farewell to costly, bulky, room-sized simulators and say hello to the Head Mounted Virtual Reality Display solution. Our superior simulation software coupled along with the next generation hardware, provides extensive immersion and believability, and succeeds in increasing the heartbeat of trainees. This can turn training into a fruitful realization within the touch of a button.
