    About

    Learn More About Us

    Lux & Decibels is one of the top most players in the planning & commissioning of Son et Lumiere, Intelligent Lighting Systems, Musical fountains and Sound solutions in India. With the recent boom in AR VR Technology, Lux & Decibels has diversified its services providing end to end extended reality solutions for Defence and Private organizations. It is the key to experiencing, feeling and touching the past, present and future.
    Our customized and technical expertise results in enhanced efficiency, profitability with a complete digital revolution. We deliver superior value for our customers in their digital journey with truly immersive experiences for a smart future. We special in defence training simulators - Unarmed high-risk simulation, virtual naval simulation, virtual flight simulations. From that day on we have consistently delivered several challenging projects for defence clients.
    Lux & Decibels team is a multidisciplinary team of Engineers, Technologists, Artists, Senior Digital Experts and Consultants with many years of experience across various trades. We participate in listening to your briefs and challenges in order to evaluate and design solutions in a complete and sustainable package for a brightertomorrow. One of our first project with Virtual Reality Operator Training Simulator (VR-OTS) was PISTOL AND ASSAULT RIFLE FIRING RANGE for the armed forces. Learn More

    Shiva Pillai

    Principal Consultant
    The changes and uncertainty brought on by the COVID-19 pandemic upset the order of many people’s lives. But the truth is every situation in life gives us a purpose to survive and an opportunity to excel. L&D is the brainchild of Mr. Shiva Prasad Pillai, a BSc Mathematics graduate and MBA from University of Madras, with two decades of experience in Intelligent light and sound. He was awarded the Lighting designer of the year in 2006 and the best Principal Consultant at WBTDC for sustained glitch free project ideation, visualization, and execution. It was his vision and the team’s support, which initiated the onset of an ARVR company, 2 years prior to the lockdown.

    Bart Hemelaer

    Robotics Engineer
    Bart Hemelaer, a Robotics Engineer who started off in 1993 as a lighting designer in Belgium, providing design and execution of many pubs, discotheques and Music festivals. He was hired by Martin Professional lighting and posted in India as CTO to reorganize the technical operations for them at the age of 25. He was solely responsible for the Channel V music awards, Michael Jacksons Tour in India and many projects of repute. Today Bart is the International Principal consultant for L&D implementing new techniques, company segments and divisions.

    Ravi Kumar R

    CEO

    Started his career with a passion for events from college days itself in the year 1997, was keen on event production and technical aspect front along with artists management with one of the oldest event management company from South India . Later joined corporate house Sahara India as Head-Events & Sports for the entire group of companies including Events, Sports verticals, Entertainment, Real Estate, Hospitality and other businesses on PAN India & International as well. Been a senior management personal in the organisation for 15 years and have handled IPL, Hockey India League, Badminton League, Share Force India to name name a few. Now a freelance consultant in the industry of entertainment, sports, VR & AR, sound and light handling projects across India & International projects. Experience of more then 25 years of industry experience

    Purnima Nayanar

    Creative Head

    Purnima Nayanar, is a fashion designer from NIFT, Bangalore, with an absolute creative slant and love for design space ever since her graduation. In this ever-evolving virtual world of design, her journey began as a Fashion designer in the vibrant garment industry at first and then as a Graphics designer, before helping to set up and develop the business of complex lighting specific designs at CGI. Her repertoire of visuals specifics and extended reality include and is not limited to complex animations, 3D working model and graphic rendering. She heads the creative department of L&D.

    Yugander RJ

    Chief Design Consultant

    Yugander RJ, B.E in Electrical & Electronic Communication, started his career in photography, movie editing and cinematography, at quite a young age. His keenness was recognized by a Singapore channel News Asia, where he worked as the Creative Designer and Editor. He specializes in 3Ds Max, Maya, Substance Painter, Autodesk CAD, Revit, Avid, F.C.P and Adobe collections. “As technology becomes more and more accessible, AR VR is the next step to many businesses”, says designer Yugander RJ. He is currently the Chief Design Consultant of L&D.

    Rishab B
