    Lux & Decibels is one of the top most players in the planning & commissioning of Son et Lumiere, Intelligent Lighting Systems, Musical fountains and Sound solutions in India. With the recent boom in AR VR Technology, Lux & Decibels has diversified its services providing end to end extended reality solutions for Defence and Private organizations. It is the key to experiencing, feeling and touching the past, present and future.
    Our customized and technical expertise results in enhanced efficiency, profitability with a complete digital revolution. We deliver superior value for our customers in their digital journey with truly immersive experiences for a smart future. We special in defence training simulators - Unarmed high-risk simulation, virtual naval simulation, virtual flight simulations. From that day on we have consistently delivered several challenging projects for defence clients.
    Lux & Decibels team is a multidisciplinary team of Engineers, Technologists, Artists, Senior Digital Experts and Consultants with many years of experience across various trades. We participate in listening to your briefs and challenges in order to evaluate and design solutions in a complete and sustainable package for a brighter tomorrow. One of our first project with Virtual Reality Operator Training Simulator (VR-OTS) was PISTOL AND ASSAULT RIFLE FIRING RANGE for the armed forces.
